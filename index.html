Welcome to Explore Log!

This is a tool to visualize your location history using Leaflet and your data collected from Google Timeline. The map is covered in "fog" and places you've visited will be revealed (similar to "fog of war" in video games).

To download the Location History.json file:
1. Go to Google Takeout
2. Click 'Deselect all'
3. Scroll down until you find Location History and mark the checkbox
4. Click 'Next Step', then 'Create Archive'
5. Download the file
